Atari and video games at 80s

Atari took the world by storm and began the video arcade industry in 1972, when it released the arcade game Pong. Five years later in 1977, the company once again changed the face of entertainment when it introduced its first home video game console or VCS (video computer system). While Atari’s cartridge-based game console took off with phenomenal success, it wasn’t actually the first to market.
